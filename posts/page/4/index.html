May 22, 2014

Competition Season Overview

Competition Season Overview We competed in the Oregon City competition. We had some problems with our robot in the first few qualification matches, but the team worked together to solve them. After improving in the next few matches, we were selected to be on an alliance. Unfortunately, we lost our first semifinal round and were eliminated from the competition. We finished in 25th place out of 35 teams! Overall, we are proud of our efforts as a team and plan to do even better in our Wilsonville competition this weekend!
